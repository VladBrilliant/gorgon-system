# Gorgon System ‚Äî Code of Conduct

Gorgon System is an open research and engineering project focused on bio-inspired
monitoring, diagnostics and adaptive intelligence. All participants, contributors
and users are expected to maintain a respectful, constructive and safe environment.

This Code of Conduct defines the standards of behaviour for all interactions
within the project, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions,
documentation, and any other project-related communication.

---

## üå± 1. Our Principles

- Treat everyone with **respect**, regardless of skill level, background or opinions.
- Assume **good intentions** when reading messages or reviewing code.
- Focus criticism on **ideas**, not people.
- Communicate **clearly, calmly and constructively**.
- Help create an environment where contributors feel **safe to ask questions**.
- Value diversity of thought, skill level and experience.

---

## üö´ 2. Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviour is **not permitted**:

- Harassment, insults, threats or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or hateful language toward any group.
- Sexist, racist, xenophobic or derogatory remarks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Persistent disruptive behaviour, trolling or derailing discussions.
- Spam, advertising or unrelated promotional content.
- Intentionally submitting malicious code.

Project maintainers may warn, restrict or remove individuals who engage in such behaviour.

## üõ† 4. Enforcement

If you witness behaviour that violates this Code:

1. You may open an issue (if you feel comfortable).
2. You may contact the project owner privately via the email listed on the GitHub profile.
3. Reports will be reviewed carefully, respectfully and confidentially.

Violations may result in:

- a warning,
- temporary removal from participation,
- blocking from the repository,
- or other actions deemed appropriate by the maintainer.

The primary goal of enforcement is **education, improvement and safety**, not punishment.
